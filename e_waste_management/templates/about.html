About Us

EnviroServe Kenya aims to address these challenge of improper electronic disposal by introducing an innovative website-based e-waste collection service. This platform will allow individuals and businesses to schedule pickups for responsible disposal of electronic devices, ensuring environmentally-friendly recycling practices. Through this service, we aim to reach a wider population, educate the public on sustainable recycling, and help reduce the negative environmental impact of e-waste.
